10-05 Module Summary
1. Overview

The AI Intelligence & Governance Module provides a secure, scalable, and provider-independent Artificial Intelligence framework for LifeChronicle Version 1.0.

AI enhances the application through intelligent assistance, reporting, analytics, and administrative support while always respecting privacy, security, and user control.

2. Key Features

The module provides:

AI Assistance
AI Reports & Analytics
AI Governance
AI Safety
AI Monitoring
Provider-Independent Architecture
Configuration-Driven AI
Master Data Prompt Management
3. Design Principles

The module follows:

AI Assists, Users Decide
Configuration Before Code
Privacy Before Intelligence
Provider Independence
Human Override
AI as an Optional Service

The AI Intelligence & Governance Module establishes a centralized and future-ready AI framework that delivers intelligent assistance, configurable automation, privacy-aware processing, and seamless integration across LifeChronicle while remaining independent of any specific AI provider.
