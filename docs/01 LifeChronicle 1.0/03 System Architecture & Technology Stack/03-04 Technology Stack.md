03-04 Technology Stack
1. Introduction

This document defines the technologies selected for developing LifeChronicle Version 1.0. Each technology has been chosen based on reliability, maintainability, community support, and future scalability.

2. Purpose

The Technology Stack shall:

Standardize development.
Ensure compatibility.
Simplify maintenance.
Support future enhancements.

AI providers are not hardcoded and may be changed through future system configuration and implementation.

Stability
Scalability

Community Support

12. Summary

The selected technology stack provides a solid foundation for developing a secure, scalable, AI-ready, and enterprise-grade LifeChronicle platform while supporting future expansion without major architectural changes.
