# 01-14 Version Roadmap

## 1. Introduction

This document presents the planned evolution of LifeChronicle across future versions. It serves as a strategic roadmap rather than a fixed development schedule.

The roadmap helps prioritize development while ensuring that Version 1.0 remains stable and focused.

---

# 2. Version 1.0

## Primary Goal

Deliver a complete, stable, and well-documented digital life management platform.

### Major Deliverables

* User Authentication
* Birth Profile
* Interactive Timeline
* Event Management
* Travel Activities
* Relationship Management
* Collaborative Events
* Media Management
* Location Management
* Notification Center
* AI Intelligence
* Administration Portal
* Help Desk
* Business Rules
* Multilingual Architecture
* Master Data Management
* Analytics Dashboard
* Complete Documentation

---

# 3. Version 1.x

Future minor releases may include:

* Performance Improvements
* UI Enhancements
* Additional AI Rules
* New Reports
* More Languages
* Security Enhancements
* Additional Validation Rules
* User Experience Improvements

---

# 4. Version 2.0

Potential enhancements include:

* Mobile Applications
* Video Support
* Audio Memories
* OCR Integration
* Calendar Synchronization
* Cloud Storage Integration
* Advanced AI Assistance
* Timeline Insights
* Premium Features

---

# 5. Version 3.0

Long-term possibilities:

* Enterprise Edition
* Team Collaboration
* AI Life Analytics
* Smart Memory Discovery
* Advanced Search
* Cross-Platform Synchronization
* Third-Party Integrations

---

# 6. Development Strategy

Every future version should:

* Preserve architectural consistency.
* Maintain backward compatibility whenever practical.
* Extend existing modules rather than replacing them.
* Follow documentation-first development.

---

# 7. Summary

The roadmap provides a structured path for the continued evolution of LifeChronicle while protecting the stability and quality of Version 1.0.
