02-28 Personal Reports & Export

1. Introduction

The Personal Reports & Export module enables users to generate reports, summaries, and statistics based on their own LifeChronicle data.

Reports help users review, analyze, preserve, and share selected parts of their digital life while maintaining complete ownership and privacy of their information.

This module is intended for personal reporting only. Administrative reports are covered separately in 02-30 Reports & Analytics.

2. Purpose

The module shall:

Generate personal reports.

Provide timeline statistics.

Summarize life events.

Export personal information.

Support AI-generated summaries.

Preserve personal memories in printable formats.

3. Actors

Primary Actor:

Registered User

Secondary Actors:

Artificial Intelligence
System

4. Functional Requirements

Users shall be able to:

Generate reports.

Preview reports.

Download reports.

Print reports.

Share reports (subject to privacy settings).

Regenerate reports after data changes.

8. Business Rules

Users may generate reports only from their own accessible data.

Privacy settings shall always be respected.

Generated reports shall reflect the current data at the time of generation.

AI-generated summaries are informational and do not modify user data.

9. AI Assistance

AI may assist by:

Generating life summaries.

Creating timeline overviews.

Highlighting significant milestones.

Summarizing travel history.

Summarizing relationship history.

Identifying personal trends and statistics.

AI-generated reports are optional and intended to assist users in understanding their own data.

10. Validation Rules

The system shall validate:

User authentication.

Report permissions.

Selected filters.

Export format availability.

11. Error Handling

Examples include:

No data available for the selected report.

Invalid filter criteria.

Export generation failed.

Unsupported export format.

The application shall display user-friendly messages and log technical errors internally.

12. Future Enhancements

Future versions may support:

Scheduled Report Generation.

Email Delivery of Reports.

Interactive Dashboards.

AI Life Insights.

Anniversary Reports.

Family Reports.

Custom Report Builder.

13. Summary

The Personal Reports & Export module enables users to generate, analyze, export, and preserve meaningful summaries of their digital life. It provides flexible reporting, multiple export formats, AI-assisted summaries, and comprehensive filtering while ensuring privacy, security, and complete user ownership of personal information.
