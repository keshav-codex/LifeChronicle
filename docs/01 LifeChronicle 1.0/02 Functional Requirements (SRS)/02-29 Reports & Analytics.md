02-29 Reports & Analytics

1. Introduction

The Reports & Analytics module provides statistical insights for administrators through dashboards, reports, charts, and AI-assisted analysis.

The module supports operational monitoring and strategic decision-making.

2. Purpose

The module shall:

Display operational statistics.

Generate reports.

Visualize trends.

Support AI-assisted analytics.

3. Actors

Primary Actors:

Super Admin

Admin

Department Head

Department Manager

4. Functional Requirements

The module shall provide reports for:

Users

Staff

Departments

Events

Relationships

Concerns

Categories

AI Usage

Storage Usage

System Activity

5. Dashboard Analytics

The dashboard shall support:

Statistical Cards

Trend Reports

Monthly Reports

Department Reports

Staff Performance

Concern Statistics

Analytics shall utilize:

Pandas

Matplotlib

6. Business Rules

Reports respect administrative permissions.

Department Managers view only department data.

Sensitive information follows privacy rules.

7. AI Assistance

AI may:

Detect trends.

Generate summaries.

Recommend improvements.

Highlight anomalies.

AI reports remain advisory.

8. Validation Rules

The system shall validate:

Reporting permissions.

Date ranges.

Department access.

9. Error Handling

Examples:

Report unavailable.

Invalid date range.

Permission denied.

10. Future Enhancements

Interactive Dashboards.

Predictive Analytics.

Export to Excel.

Export to PDF.

11. Summary

Reports & Analytics enables administrators to monitor application health and performance through data-driven dashboards and AI-assisted insights.
