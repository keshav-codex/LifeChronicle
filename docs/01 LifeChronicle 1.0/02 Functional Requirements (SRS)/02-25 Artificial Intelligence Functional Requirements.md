02-25 Artificial Intelligence Functional Requirements

1. Introduction

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) module provides intelligent assistance throughout LifeChronicle while ensuring that users retain complete ownership and control of their content.

AI acts as an assistant, advisor, reviewer, and governance tool. It never replaces user decisions.

2. Purpose

The AI module shall:

Improve content quality.

Assist users during data entry.

Recommend better organization.

Support administrators.

Detect harmful or policy-violating content.

Generate intelligent reports.

3. Actors

Primary Actors:

Registered User

Administration

Secondary Actor:

Artificial Intelligence Engine

4. AI Functional Areas

AI shall support:

Event Description Improvement

Category Recommendation

Subcategory Recommendation

Timeline Improvement

Birth Profile Improvement

Relationship Assistance

Administrative Assistance

Report Generation

Content Moderation

5. AI Suggestions

AI Suggestions are optional.

Users may:

View Suggestions.

Accept Suggestions.

Reject Suggestions.

AI shall never modify user-generated content automatically.

AI shall recommend improvements only when meaningful improvements exist.

6. AI Alerts & Warnings

AI may generate alerts for:

Harmful Content.

Offensive Language.

Illegal Content.

Policy Violations.

Unsafe Images.

Security Risks.

Alerts require user attention but do not automatically remove content unless required by system policy or administrative action.

7. Administrative AI

AI may assist administrators by:

Detecting duplicate categories.

Suggesting new categories.

Detecting unusual activity.

Assisting concern classification.

Identifying frequently reported issues.

Generating analytical reports.

Administrative decisions always require human approval.

8. Business Rules

AI is advisory only.

Users retain full ownership of their content.

AI shall not publish, edit, or delete content automatically.

AI decisions affecting users require human review where applicable.

AI prompts and rules are managed through Master Data and support Excel Import/Export.

9. Validation Rules

The system shall validate:

AI permissions.

User permissions.

AI rule availability.

Prompt configuration.

10. Error Handling

Examples:

AI service unavailable.

Suggestion generation failed.

Prompt configuration missing.

AI timeout.

The application shall continue functioning even when AI services are unavailable.

11. Future Enhancements

AI Timeline Summaries.

AI Memory Insights.

AI Life Reports.

AI Semantic Search.

AI Voice Assistant.

Personalized AI Recommendations.

12. Summary

The AI module enhances the LifeChronicle experience by providing intelligent recommendations, administrative assistance, and safety monitoring while ensuring that users remain in complete control of their personal data and decisions.
