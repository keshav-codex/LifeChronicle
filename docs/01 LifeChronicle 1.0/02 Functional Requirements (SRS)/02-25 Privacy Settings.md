02-25 Privacy Settings
1. Introduction

The Privacy Settings module allows users to control who can view their personal information, Birth Profile, timeline, events, relationships, media, and other content within LifeChronicle.

LifeChronicle follows a Privacy First philosophy, where users retain ownership and control over their personal information.

2. Purpose

The module shall:

Protect user privacy.
Allow configurable visibility.
Control access to personal information.
Secure shared content.
Support future privacy enhancements.
3. Actors

Primary Actor:

Registered User

Secondary Actor:

System
4. Functional Requirements

Users shall be able to configure privacy for:

Birth Profile
Timeline
Individual Events
Relationships
Media
Locations
Collaborative Events
5. Privacy Levels

The application shall support:

Only Me
Specific Relationships
All Relationships
Public (where applicable)

Additional privacy options may be introduced in future versions.

6. Business Rules
Privacy settings apply immediately.
Individual event privacy overrides timeline privacy.
Users cannot bypass another user's privacy settings.
Privacy changes are recorded in audit logs.
7. AI Assistance

AI may:

Recommend stronger privacy settings.
Detect potentially overexposed information.
Suggest privacy improvements.

AI recommendations remain optional.

8. Validation Rules

The system shall validate:

Privacy ownership.
User permissions.
Relationship permissions.
9. Error Handling

Examples:

Permission denied.
Privacy configuration unavailable.
Invalid visibility selection.
10. Future Enhancements
Temporary Sharing.
Link-based Sharing.
Time-based Privacy.
AI Privacy Advisor.
11. Summary

The Privacy Settings module ensures that users maintain complete control over who can access their personal information while supporting flexible, configuration-driven privacy management.
